Lord of the Rings is awesome

Know some characters

Tolkien not only created an engaging plot, but developed a complete mythology, with different peoples with their own languages, cultures, religions, genealogies, and deeply intertwined histories.

Gandalf the Grey, a Wizard. Wise, powerful and kind. He is known for his deep knowledge of lore and magic, his bravery in battle, and his commitment to fighting evil.

Frodo Baggins, a Hobbit. Courageous, determined and compassionate. Frodo is the one chosen to carry the One Ring to Mount Doom to destroy it.

Legolas Greenleaf, an Sindar Elf. Agile and keen-eyed. He is known for his unmatched archery skills, his ability to move silently and swiftly.

Lady Galadriel, an ancient Elf. Wise, powerful and ethereal. One of the most powerful Elves in Middle-earth. She possesses great beauty, wisdom and magical abilities.
